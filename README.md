# AI-Assisted Food Safety Risk Assessment Workflow

## Project Overview

This project explores how structured prompt engineering and AI-assisted scientific reasoning can support contamination risk assessment in food production systems.

The workflow focuses on food microbiology, HACCP logic, contamination pathways, environmental monitoring, and structured risk characterization relevant to industrial food safety systems.

The project is designed as an educational and portfolio-oriented scientific workflow prototype aligned with applied food technology and food safety analysis.

---

## Objectives

- Develop structured contamination risk assessment workflows
- Apply HACCP-oriented reasoning to food production systems
- Analyze contamination pathways and environmental risks
- Organize scientific outputs using standardized frameworks
- Demonstrate applied food microbiology systems thinking

---

## Scientific Scope

This workflow focuses on:

- Food microbiology
- Food safety systems
- HACCP principles
- Environmental monitoring programs (EMP)
- Contamination pathway analysis
- Detection methods
- Risk characterization
- Corrective and preventive controls
